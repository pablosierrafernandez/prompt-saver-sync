Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 20, 2025

Data Storage & Synchronization

By default, all prompts you save are stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API. This data remains on your device and is never transmitted to any external servers unless you choose to synchronize.

Google Drive Sync (Optional)

When you enable Google Drive synchronization, our extension requests permission to create and manage a single file named prompt_saver_data.json in your Google Drive. This file contains only your saved prompts. All communication with Google Drive is encrypted, and we only access the file we create, not your entire Drive.
